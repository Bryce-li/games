About the Game

Leap and Avoid 2 is an exhilarating endless runner that will test your reflexes to the limit. In this fast-paced game, you control a character that automatically runs forward through an ever-changing obstacle course.

Your goal is simple yet challenging: survive as long as possible by jumping over obstacles and sliding under threats. The game features increasingly difficult levels that will push your timing and reaction skills to their limits.

Game Features

Endless Runner

Enjoy infinite gameplay with procedurally generated levels that keep getting more challenging.

Simple Controls

Easy to learn one-touch controls make it accessible for players of all ages and skill levels.

Increasing Difficulty

The game becomes progressively harder as you advance, testing your reflexes with complex obstacle patterns.

Retro Style

Features a charming pixel art style that brings back memories of classic arcade games while feeling fresh and modern.

How to Play

Playing Leap and Avoid 2 is straightforward but mastering it takes practice:

  • Jump: Tap or press the spacebar to jump over obstacles
  • Slide: Swipe down or press the down arrow to duck under threats
  • Collect Coins: Gather coins during gameplay to unlock special characters and power-ups
  • Avoid Obstacles: Stay alive as long as possible to achieve high scores
